About Ejaar

Bridging the Gap in Bahrain's Rental Market

In the real estate market, there is limited digitalization of local rental/leasing processes and an absence of a unified platform to support online payments, transparent listings, and maintenance reporting.

Ejaar, a centralized rental platform for Bahrain, was developed to bridge this gap and provide landlords with a tool that allows them to have direct access to renters without the need for a middleman.

The work involved in this project spans both business and technology. An in-depth business plan was conducted to understand what the market needs and how Ejaar can position itself. The app was developed to be user-friendly, efficient, and effective.
